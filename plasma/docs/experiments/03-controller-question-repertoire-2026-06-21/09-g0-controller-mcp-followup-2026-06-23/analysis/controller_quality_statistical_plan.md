# Controller-quality Statistical Plan

Design: paired mission-seed blocks with cells C0, V2, V3, and AUTO. First formal
look requires at least 8 complete clean blocks. Target is 12 blocks, capped at 16
unless a separate power review approves more.

Primary product candidate: AUTO versus C0.

Success requires both:

- AUTO improves or ties C0 on readability, depth, or breadth in paired analysis.
- AUTO is non-inferior on grounding/provenance safety thresholds.

Diagnostic cells V2 and V3 explain selector behavior but are not standalone
product defaults.
